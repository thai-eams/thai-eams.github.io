TinyTEAMS: Sonic Ventures

7 February 2026
Goethe Saal, Goethe-Institut Thailand

This edition serves as the concluding event of the workshop “Sonic Ventures: Into the New (Sound) World”, reflecting its focus on electroacoustic sound practices from the past, present, and future.

The concert features fixed-media works, live electronics, and audiovisual performances by emerging artists, professional practitioners, and electroacoustic pioneers, with works by CRSRCRSRRR, MahakitMahaniranon, Yishi Zhong, Sippapas Thienwiwat, and others.

The concert is organised by TEAMS and curated by Sippapas Thienwiwat, with the aim of expanding public access and awareness of electroacoustic music in Thailand through an intimate and exploratory listening experience.

– Sippapas Thienwiwat
TEAMS President
