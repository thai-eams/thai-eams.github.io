Sippapas Thienwiwat's portrait concert as part of Tropical Glitch @ MUSIC PROJECT

11 July 2026
Bangkok CityCity Gallery

This portrait concert, although a tad bit short as it is presented as part of the Tropical Glitch commissioned talk series, focuses. The program features 2 acousmatic works that have inspired me as of lately, and 3 recent works of mine which I hold dear to my heart.

– Sippapas Thienwiwat
TEAMS President
