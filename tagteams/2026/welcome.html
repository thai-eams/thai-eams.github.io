Message from our board members

TEAMS President

On behalf of my beloved TEAMS members, welcome to TagTEAMS 2026.

What began as a bold idea has become a living, resonant reality. Presented by the Thai ElectroAcoustic Music Society (TEAMS), this inaugural edition transforms the heart of Bangkok’s Chinatown into a landscape of sound, where narrow streets vibrate with new frequencies and familiar spaces are reimagined through listening.

TagTEAMS 2026 is a meeting of worlds, bringing together immersive artworks, radical sonic experiments, technological spectacles, and quiet moments of deep attention. With over 70 artists from across the globe, this festival is not merely a program of performances. It is an invitation to experience sound as architecture, as memory, and as future.

Here, cultures intersect through loudspeakers and reflect in space.
Here, ideas echo beyond language.
Here, a new community gathers, curious, daring, and ready to listen differently.

Thank you for joining us at the beginning of what we hope will be an enduring resonance.

Enjoy the journey, and welcome to the TEAMS!

– Sippapas Thienwiwat
TEAMS President & Festival Artistic Director

BEAST Director

Speaking on behalf of myself and my colleagues at Birmingham ElectroAcoustic Sound Theatre (BEAST), we're delighted to be playing a part in the inaugural edition of TagTEAMS. Founded over 40 years ago, BEAST must now be one of the oldest running organisations for spatialised electroacoustic music in the world, so what a pleasure to be present at the formal launch of one of the newest, and see all the imagination, enthusiasm, and collaborative spirit TEAMS is bringing to their activities. I hope that this will provide a productive focal point for the range of exciting music happening in Thailand, the region, and beyond, and that it will thrive for years to come.

– Scott Wilson
BEAST Co-director
